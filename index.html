Project Synopsis

This project was inspired by the need to provide a one-stop online shop for men's products such as clothing. This application is dedicated to the men's niche hence is very convenient. Most of the already existing solutions have all manner of products for the genearl population causing a loss of time as males search for their preffered products. We solve that problem through M_Store, by the way M stands for Males, a one-stop, convenient store.
